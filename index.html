About Dr. Sarah Johnson

Dedicated to providing exceptional dental care and creating beautiful smiles for over 15 years.

Expertise & Experience

Dr. Sarah Johnson graduated with honors from the University of Michigan School of Dentistry and has been practicing for over 15 years. She regularly pursues continuing education to stay at the forefront of dental technology and techniques.

Philosophy of Care

Dr. Johnson believes in a patient-centered approach, taking time to listen to concerns and creating customized treatment plans. She is known for her gentle touch and ability to make even the most anxious patients feel comfortable.

Advanced Technology

Our practice is equipped with the latest dental technology including digital X-rays, intraoral cameras, and laser dentistry to provide efficient, comfortable, and precise treatments.
